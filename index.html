A JOURNEY TO AFRICA'S CULTURAL HEART

After a successful debut at Expo 2020 in Dubai, where we served over 150,000 meals with a modern, fusion, and internationally-oriented format, it became clear that we needed to reinvent ourselves for Expo 2025 Osaka by creating a bespoke "African Food Market" experience.

For Osaka, we are returning to the fundamentals, highlighting the essence of Africa, its culture, and its rich heritage. This new format is centered on human connections, where the world meets Africa in a vibrant, communal atmosphere that celebrates the continent's traditions and diversity.

Meet Panaf' - The African Food Market

An immersive culinary experience at Expo 2025 Osaka, celebrating Africa's diverse flavors and vibrant market culture.

Savor the Essence

Discover Africa's rich culinary heritage, where each food counter is a gateway to a different region, offering an authentic taste of its unique flavors and traditions.

Mabrouk (North Africa)

From hearty tagines to refreshing couscous salads, this counter promises a feast for the senses.

Baobab Bazaar (West Africa)

Enjoy jollof rice, suya, and other beloved West African staples that showcase the region's vibrant food culture.

Bantu Bistro (Central Africa)

Savor hearty, flavorful dishes featuring traditional stews, grilled meats, and other comforting fare.

Rift Valley (Horn of Africa)

From injera with doro wat to savory sambusas, this counter offers a taste of the Horn's rich culinary traditions.

Tinga Foods (East Africa)

Explore fresh, flavorful dishes such as grilled meats, spiced rice dishes, and vibrant vegetable stews.

Ubuntu Grill (Southern Africa)

This counter offers a range of grilled meats, seafood dishes, and savory pastries.

Creole Corner (Indian Ocean)

Expect a fusion of African, Asian, and European influences, resulting in unique and tantalizing dishes.
